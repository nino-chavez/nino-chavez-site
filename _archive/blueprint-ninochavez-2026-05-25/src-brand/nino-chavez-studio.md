## Voice & Tone

### Attributes
- **playful**: Fun-first but competitive — never corny
  > "Bring your friends. Leave your egos. Play some grass volleyball."
- **heat-themed**: Uses pepper/spice metaphors consistently
  > "This tournament is a Jalapeño — not too hot, not too mild."
- **community-driven**: Celebrates the players, not the organizers
  > "12 teams showed up. Nobody went home disappointed."
- **direct**: Short, punchy, no corporate filler
  > "3v3. Grass. $150/team. Let's go."

### Avoid
- **corporate-sports** (error): exciting opportunity, amazing community, we're thrilled to announce, register today for an unforgettable experience
  > Instead: Talk like a player, not a marketing department.
- **over-explaining** (warning): grass triples is a format where, for those who don't know, in case you're wondering
  > Instead: The audience knows volleyball. Don't explain the basics.

### Examples
**Announce a tournament**
- Good: "New drop: Jalapeño Series, April 12th. 3v3 grass. Waveland Park. $150/team. Limited to 16 teams."
- Bad: "We're excited to announce our next amazing grass volleyball tournament! Join us for an incredible day of competition and community!"

**Describe a heat level**
- Good: "Ghost Pepper is invite-only. If you have to ask, you're not ready yet."
- Bad: "Our Ghost Pepper tier is designed for advanced competitive players seeking a challenging tournament environment."
